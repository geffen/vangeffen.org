Increased marketing ROI for Sephora by designing, programming, and hosting promotional micro-sites campaigns for three years. These sites topped out at registering more than 1.5 million users!

Built an administration and call center page for Sephora with active metrics for real-time reporting on emails captured, codes released, quantity of promotional products, and employee appreciation metrics.

At Sephora, I had the privilege of contributing to one of the world's leading beauty retailers, where I played a pivotal role in enhancing the online customer experience. My responsibilities included collaborating on designs and the full implementation of microsites, including hosting and analytics. During my time with Sephora, I gained valuable insights into the beauty industry, honed my skills in web usability, ajax web programming, and had the opportunity to collaborate with a talented team of professionals who share a passion for innovation and customer satisfaction.

Together, we worked towards ensuring that Sephora remained at the forefront of the beauty industry, providing customers with not only exceptional products but also an immersive and delightful shopping experience

Approach

  • Wireframes

    Collaborating with a company involves a dynamic interplay of client communication, brand identity consistency, and a strong commitment to collaborative work. It's a delicate dance, where understanding the client's vision is paramount. Open channels of communication facilitate the alignment of design with their goals, ensuring that each project is a reflection of their brand identity. The key lies in harmonizing the creative process with their unique values, resulting in designs that not only meet their expectations but elevate their brand, all while fostering a truly collaborative partnership.

  • Digital Design

    Sephora's brand identity is the guiding star in creating designs that are not only responsive across various platforms but also, and crucially, fun and exciting in the realm of gaming. This delicate balance between brand consistency and captivating gameplay is where design innovation flourishes. It's about seamlessly incorporating Sephora's visual language into the gaming world, ensuring that the brand essence shines through while also delivering immersive and enjoyable experiences that resonate with users on multiple platforms.

  • Real-time Analytic Dashboard

    In addition to upholding brand identity and delivering engaging gameplay, our team tackled the formidable task of handling millions of hits, distributing prize codes, optimizing stock management, and developing a real-time dashboard for user tracking. This multifaceted approach combined meticulous back-end processes with user-focused front-end interactions, ensuring that every click and interaction seamlessly contributed to a rewarding and streamlined user experience.
